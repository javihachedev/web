I worked as **Project Manager and Team Leader** for the software system supporting the user services of the Spanish Earth observation satellite **PAZ**. In this role, I was responsible for overseeing the **maintenance phase of the operational software**, ensuring **service continuity, reliability, and performance** for end users relying on satellite data products. Given the mission-critical nature of the system, stability, traceability, and controlled evolution were fundamental aspects of my approach.

During the maintenance phase, I coordinated **corrective, adaptive, and preventive activities**, prioritizing incidents while maintaining **strict quality and configuration control standards**. I led the technical team in diagnosing and resolving complex issues, ensuring **minimal disruption to operational services**. This required close coordination between development, operations, and stakeholders, as well as structured planning to guarantee compliance with service-level agreements and contractual commitments.

In parallel, I managed extension contracts aimed at incorporating new features and service capabilities into the system. I supervised **requirements analysis, effort estimation, planning, and execution**, ensuring that new developments integrated seamlessly into the existing operational environment without compromising system stability.

As both project manager and team leader, I balanced **technical oversight with people management**. I coordinated workloads, supported team members in **technical decision-making**, and ensured alignment between business objectives and engineering implementation. Regular communication with stakeholders and institutional representatives was a key part of the role, providing visibility into progress, risks, and strategic direction.

This experience strengthened my ability to*lead software projects in operational aerospace contexts, where reliability, contractual rigor, and controlled evolution are as important as technical excellence. It also reinforced my commitment to delivering **high-quality engineering solutions** while keeping long-term maintainability and service continuity.
